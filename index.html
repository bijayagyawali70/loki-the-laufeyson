Loki is a character of MCU. Loki is prince of Asgard,son of Odin and brother of Thor.Loki was born a Frost Giant and abandoned as an infant by his father Laufey, only to be found by Odin during an invasion of the realm of the Frost Giants in Jotunheim. Odin used magic to make Loki appear Asgardian and raised him as a son alongside Odin's biological son, Thor.

Loki cast as a negative character in MCU,Now the positive side of Loki can be seen in Loki Series releasing on Disney+.
